PERFORMANCE

CT 100 comes with a revolutionary 97.2 cc single cylinder, air cooled engine which churns out 8.2 Ps power and delivers an outstanding mileage of 99.1 kmpl*.

COMFORT

SNS SUSPENSION
CT 100 comes with SNS (spring in spring suspension) which gives outstanding ability to handle bad roads with comfort. It uses two springs; an inner and an outer one of the same axis but of different dimensions. These springs differentiate between large and small shocks. While the inner spring absorbs the impact of small bumps the outer spring absorbs the impact of bigger ones.
Long Seat
CT 100's extra-long seat provides sufficient space and comfort for both rider and pillion.

SAFETY

CT 100 comes with wheelbase of 1235 mm & 110 mm drum brakes which ensures a stable ride on all kind of roads. 3 inch wide rear tyres provide amazing road grip and superb handling. Solid build with 108 Kg Kerb weight

STYLING
