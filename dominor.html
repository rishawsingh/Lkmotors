Features

The bike got a chunky USD fork, front radial brake caliper and a DOHC setup, just like the KTM 390 Duke. The powerful LED headlight remains as do the other LED illumination bits. You get two digital information panels on the Dominar 400, the secondary one mounted on the fuel tank. The primary one portrays information such as the speedometer, the tachometer, real-time and average fuel consumption indicator, a fuel gauge, and a side-stand warning. The secondary panel has trip meters, odometer, a gear position indicator, and a clock.
