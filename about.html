In business since 2006, L K Motors in Naxalbari, Siliguri is a prominent dealer for Bajaj two-wheelers. It is an integral part of the network of Bajaj motorcycle dealerships managed by Bajaj Auto Limited. A home-grown automotive brand, Bajaj Auto is a key entity and the automotive arm of the Bajaj Group, an acclaimed Indian business conglomerate. The Bajaj brand was built over the years and has earned an indomitable repute for offering highest quality products at affordable prices. This automotive brand has succeeded in forging a relationship of trust with its growing base of customers and its numerous authorised dealers located in the various parts of the country.

Part of this growing dealership network, is this dealer showroom who has come to be recognised as one of the preferred Bajaj motorcycle dealers in the region.

In the neighbourhood of Naxalbari which is well-connected by various modes of transport, lies this dealership on Naxalbari Main Road,Opp Primary School.

L K Motors in Naxalbari, Siliguri is a spacious air-conditioned showroom space that welcomes visitors anytime as per their convenience from 09:00 - 19:00. Prospective buyers and visitors are promptly attended to by the sales and service representatives. Our showroom proudly displays some of the most popular and best-selling models and variants of motorcycles and motorbikes including Avenger, Bajaj V, Discover, Dominar, Pulsar, Platina and CT 100. The staff is well-versed with the specifications, features, availability and finance options available and assist buyers in choosing their dream ride.

Customers can also seamlessly avail a myriad of value added services for their two-wheelers, including bike customizations and accessories, bike repair and care, insurance buying and renewal, and service booking.

One can conveniently pay for the products and services by Cash, Master Card, Visa Card, Debit Cards, Cheques, Credit Card. The two-wheeler finance options available also make it convenient for a customer to own their dream ride.
